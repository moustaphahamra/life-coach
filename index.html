Meet Coach Marwan Asmar

Marwan Asmar is a Business and Life coach, Director of Civility Experts Lebanon, Initiator & President of Role play NGO, and Founder of “M.A. Powers” & “La Maison de l’étiquette”. Following more than 12 years of experience in the M.I.C.E industry, his devotion and passion pushed him to win the International Civility Award and become a World Civility Ambassador.

Asmar was capable of changing numerous amounts of career paths through his coaching services and trainings, yielding people to become influential leaders in their societies, organizations, and business workplace.
